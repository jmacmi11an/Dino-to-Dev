Who I was

Before becoming a software developer I spent years as a professional puppeteer. I performed in 40 countries around the world as different animals. Most notably, I was Joey the main horse in War Horse - King Kong in King Kong the musical - and Baby T-Rex in Walking with Dinosaurs. So I guess you could say I used to be a professional dinosaur.

Who I am

I am a graduate from the Software Engineering Immersive at General Assembly. In the course of those three months I built four apps and worked my way through the basics of HTML5/CSS3 all the way through to Rails, React, Gatsby and Vue.

I am also an active member of Artists Who Code, an online community of performers turned techies and am in the process of helming their idea incubator subgroup. I am loving every minute of it so far and can't wait to continue on this journey!
